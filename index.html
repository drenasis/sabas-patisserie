About the bakery

Saba's Patisserie is a small, home-based bakery building one cake at a time — no walk-in counter, no pre-made designs on a shelf. Every order begins with a conversation about who the cake is for, and works backwards from there: the colours they like, the character they're obsessed with this month, the flavour that means something.

  • Every cake is baked fresh in the days before your event, never frozen and thawed.
  • Drips, toppers and sugar work are all handmade in-house, piece by piece.
  • Flavour pairings are built around the person it's for, not a set menu.

From enquiry to collection

How an order comes together

01

Enquire

Send over the date, headcount and the vibe — a theme, a colour, or simply "make it chocolate." A rough budget and inspiration pictures helps too.

02

Design & taste

You'll get a design and flavour pairing back within a couple of days. Tweak the details until it's exactly right.

03

Bake & collect

Your cake is baked fresh in the days before, ready to collect from Newton Mearns — well-boxed and ready to travel.
